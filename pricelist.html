Drop Off Service: You can bring your PC to our office and we'll take care of everything. Once the work is done, you can pick it up within 24 hours.

Remote Service: If you don't have time to bring your PC to us, we can connect to your computer remotely and provide support that way. You just need a stable internet connection and a computer with a webcam and microphone. We use a trusted and safe service to connect remotely and we ask that you be present during the repair process to handle any admin our router-based action that we may need.

House Call Service: If you prefer, we can come to your location and provide support there. This service is ideal for clients who are unable to transport their PCs or who simply prefer to have us come to them. An additional fee will apply. That fee is based on distance, and travel time.
