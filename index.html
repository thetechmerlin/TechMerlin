About me

My journey with technology began in the 90s, a time when dial-up modems were the norm and the internet was still a strange and mysterious world. As an artist with a penchant for creating visually stunning masterpieces on the digital canvas, I quickly realized that having a reliable, high-performance computer was absolutely essential. I witnessed firsthand how a slow or malfunctioning machine could derail my creative flow and even worse, lead to the loss of precious work.

During my years in the digital trenches, I encountered countless challenges and roadblocks along the way - everything from viruses and malware to faulty updates and user errors. My experience with going to major tech companies for help has shown me that there's a better way to provide PC maintenance and support. Companies like Best Buy and Geek Squad often promise the moon, but more often than not, their services leave clients feeling frustrated and drained of valuable time. That's why I founded Tech Merlin - to offer a more personal, efficient, and affordable alternative to the impersonal and often ineffective services provided by corporate giants.

With my extensive knowledge and experience in the world of PCs, I knew I could offer a higher level of service than what was currently available on the market. That's why I founded Tech Merlin - to provide a personal, reliable, and efficient tech service that caters to the unique needs of artists, small businesses, and anyone else who demands the very best from their technology. Whether you're in need of a quick tune-up or a full-scale system overhaul, I'm here to help you stay ahead of the curve and ensure your PC runs like a well-oiled machine.
